A consistent bedtime routine reduces anxiety and improves sleep quality. Adding a short, calm story in English makes that time special. We share how to choose the right story and keep it going without stress.

## Why a bedtime story routine works

Children do better when they know what comes next. A ritual repeated every night —bath, pyjamas, story, lights off— gives them security and helps them “wind down” from the day. If that time also includes a story in English, you’re giving two things at once: **calm** and **exposure to the language** without pressure. It’s not a lesson; it’s a moment of connection where the ear gets used to new sounds, rhythms and vocabulary naturally.

## How to choose the right story

- **Length:** For bedtime, shorter is better (5–15 minutes). That way the evening doesn’t drag and the relaxing effect stays.
- **Tone:** Calm stories, with a clear ending and no jump scares. Avoid scary or high-action plots right before turning off the light.
- **Language:** A story in English is ideal: the brain relaxes while listening and, at the same time, gets familiar with the language. If your children don’t understand everything yet, that’s fine; context and intonation help.
- **Personalisation:** When the story includes their name or their interests, attention and excitement go up. It’s “their” story.

## Three tips so it doesn’t feel like a chore

1. **Same time, same order.** It doesn’t have to be perfect every day; the story just needs to be part of the ritual most nights. Consistency matters more than perfection.
2. **You get to relax too.** If you play an audio story, you can be there with them without having to read aloud. Use that time to breathe and connect.
3. **If you skip a night, it’s okay.** You start again tomorrow. What matters is that the story is something they look forward to, not a duty that creates stress.

## One last tip

The best bedtime story is the one your child likes and that you can keep up with easily. Try short audio stories in English with a calm tone; you’ll see how that time becomes one of the nicest moments of the day.
